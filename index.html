About me

Hello and welcome to my website! I am a Political Science Ph.D. candidate at Stanford University, and my research interests include authoritarian governments, international law, human rights, and the laws of war. Before Stanford, I studied political science at UC San Diego.

As is probably apparent, I enjoy Californian weather, though I spend most of my free time in places with less comfortable climate. I recently traveled to Myanmar, the hometown of my parents. For my second-year field paper, I am investigating patterns of human rights abuses across different types of authoritarian regimes. In particular, I am interested in how the fragmentation of elite authority affects the strategies of abuse autocratic leaders use to repress their populations.
